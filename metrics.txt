precision at k is: number of relevant retrieved items in top k / k
recall at k is: number of relevant retrieved items in top k / total number of relevant items in the dataset

P@k (Precision at k) — of the top-k images the model retrieved, what fraction are actually relevant? Measures the quality  
of the ranked list. A model that returns garbage gets 0; a model that returns only relevant images gets 1.                 
                                                                                                                            
R@k (Recall at k) — of all relevant images in the gallery, what fraction appear in the top-k? Measures coverage.           
Hard-capped at k/n_relevant, so it's most meaningful when n_relevant is small relative to k. so its better for rare labels.
